Who Are We?

TAREEQ AL HARAMAIN for Hajj and Umrah Services is a travel agency that arranges tourism to the holy lands of Makkah and Madinah for the purposes of Hajj and Umrah. It provides pre-arranged groups and customized packages, including the best options for hotels, convenient transport and Umrah entry clearances for the pilgrims and visitors of both Holy Mosques in Makkah and Madinah. Not to mention reception & farewell services, together with arranging such journeys to other Islamic tours inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and worldwide.
